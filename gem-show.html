Australia's Premier Gem & Mineral Exhibition

The Diamond Valley Gem Club proudly presents our annual showcase of geological wonders, bringing together collectors, dealers, and enthusiasts from across Australia. This highly anticipated event offers visitors the opportunity to explore stunning mineral specimens, purchase unique gems, observe expert demonstrations, and participate in exciting competitions and raffles.

Whether you're a seasoned collector, budding enthusiast, or simply curious about the natural world, our show provides an educational and inspiring experience for all ages. Join us for a weekend celebrating the extraordinary beauty and diversity of minerals, gems, and lapidary arts.

Exceptional Mineral & Gem Displays

Our exhibition hall will feature breathtaking displays from distinguished collectors and dealers representing regions across Australia and internationally. View rare mineral specimens, spectacular crystal formations, ancient fossils, and exquisite examples of lapidary craftsmanship.

From Australian opals and sapphires to international varieties, witness the geological diversity that makes our planet extraordinary. Educational displays provide insights into mineral formation, identification, and the fascinating stories behind notable specimens.

Dealer Marketplace

Browse an extensive selection from trusted dealers offering rough minerals, cut gemstones, finished jewelry, lapidary equipment, and specialist tools. Whether you're seeking specimens for your collection or materials for your next project, our marketplace connects you with quality suppliers and competitive prices.

Live Demonstrations

Throughout both days, experienced club members and visiting artisans will demonstrate essential lapidary techniques including cabochon cutting, gemstone faceting, jewelry fabrication, and specimen preparation. These sessions provide valuable learning opportunities for newcomers and seasoned enthusiasts alike.

Competitions & Prize Raffles

Test your knowledge and try your luck with our popular competition categories and prize raffles. Competitions include specimen identification challenges, display competitions for club members, and photography contests celebrating the beauty of minerals and gems.

Our raffle draws feature spectacular prizes generously donated by dealers and club supporters, including quality mineral specimens, lapidary equipment, gemstones, and jewelry. All proceeds support the ongoing activities and educational programs of the Diamond Valley Gem Club.

Family-Friendly Environment

We've designed our show to be accessible and engaging for visitors of all ages. Children under 12 receive complimentary admission when accompanied by an adult, making this an ideal family outing. Interactive displays, hands-on activities, and educational materials help young visitors develop an appreciation for Earth sciences and the natural world. Our welcoming atmosphere encourages questions, learning, and discovery for everyone from first-time visitors to experienced collectors.

Visitor Information

Getting There

The Eltham Community & Reception Centre is conveniently located on Main Road in Eltham, easily accessible from Melbourne's northern suburbs and beyond. The venue offers ample free parking for all visitors. Public transport options are available with bus services stopping nearby on Main Road.

Facilities

The venue features wheelchair accessibility, accessible parking spaces, and restroom facilities. Light refreshments will be available for purchase throughout the event. We recommend bringing reusable shopping bags for any purchases you make from our dealers.

What to Bring

While card facilities are available, we recommend bringing cash as some smaller dealers may operate on a cash-only basis. If you're a collector, consider bringing reference materials or a magnifying loupe for closer specimen examination. Photography is generally permitted except where specifically restricted by dealers.

Purchasing Guidelines

All dealers at our show are carefully selected to ensure they provide authentic, properly represented merchandise. Take your time browsing, ask questions, and don't hesitate to request information about specimens or gemstones. Reputable dealers are happy to discuss their products and provide certificates of authenticity where applicable.

Questions or Further Information

For additional details about the show, special requirements, or general enquiries, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our team is happy to assist with any questions you may have.
