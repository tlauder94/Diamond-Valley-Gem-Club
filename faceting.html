About This Course

Master the precision art of gemstone faceting, transforming rough stones into brilliant, multi-faceted gems. This hands-on course covers machine setup, cutting angles, polishing techniques, and the science behind creating perfectly symmetrical gemstones.

  • Hands-on practice with faceting machines
  • Techniques for precision cutting and angle control
  • Polishing and finishing for maximum brilliance

Meet Your Instructor

Michael Stevens is a master faceter with over 25 years of experience in precision gem cutting. He has trained under internationally renowned faceters and specializes in both traditional and modern cutting techniques.

"Faceting is the art of bringing out a gemstone's true potential. Every angle matters in creating that perfect sparkle."
