Why Join Our Market?

  • Reach an engaged audience of gem and craft enthusiasts
  • Relaxed evening market atmosphere
  • Affordable stall fees
  • Supportive community environment
  • Regular event with loyal attendees
  • Free parking and easy setup

What Can You Sell?

We welcome stallholders offering gems, minerals, crystals, fossils, handmade jewelry, lapidary work, craft supplies, artisan goods, and related items. All items must be relevant to our community's interests in gems, minerals, and crafts.

Note: Stall spaces are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Early registration is recommended.

Visitor Information

Everything you need to know for your visit

Getting There

We're located at 25 Panther Place, Eltham North VIC 3095. Free parking is available on-site. The venue is easily accessible from the main road with clear signage.

What to Bring

Bring cash for purchases (some stallholders may not have card facilities), reusable bags for your treasures, and comfortable shoes for browsing.

Accessibility

Our venue is wheelchair accessible with accessible parking available. Please contact us if you have specific accessibility requirements.

COVID Safety

We follow current health guidelines to ensure a safe environment for all visitors and stallholders.
