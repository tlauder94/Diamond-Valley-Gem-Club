About This Course

Master the techniques of rock slabbing, from selecting the right stones to safely using slabbing saws. This course is ideal for anyone looking to prepare rocks for cabochons, carvings, or other lapidary projects.

  • Hands-on practice with slabbing saws
  • Techniques for selecting and preparing rough stones
  • Safety tips and maintenance of equipment

Meet Your Instructor

David Lee is a seasoned lapidary artist with over 20 years of experience in rock slabbing and preparation. He has worked with a wide variety of stones and is passionate about teaching others the fundamentals of this essential skill.
