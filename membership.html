How to Become a Member

Becoming a member of the Diamond Valley Gem Club is simple and rewarding. Follow these steps to join our vibrant community of gem enthusiasts:

  • Download the Membership Application / Renewal Form.
  • Fill out the form with your details and preferences.
  • Submit the completed form to the club in person or via email.
  • Pay the membership fee to activate your membership.

Once your membership is active, you’ll gain access to our workshops, events, and a supportive community of like-minded individuals. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced gem enthusiast, we welcome you to join us!

Why Join the Club?

The Diamond Valley Gem Club is the perfect place for anyone passionate about gems, minerals, and jewelry making. By joining the club, you gain access to a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who share your enthusiasm for exploring the beauty of gemstones.

Members enjoy access to fully equipped workshops, expert guidance from seasoned gem enthusiasts, and opportunities to participate in exclusive events and exhibitions. Joining the Diamond Valley Gem Club is more than a membership—it's an opportunity to grow your passion and skills in a supportive and inspiring setting.

Joining Fees/Subscriptions

  • Adult: $40
  • Second Adult: $30
  • Third (or more) Adult: $20
  • Child 16 - 18: $20
  • Child Under 16*: $15

Joining Fee (per person): $10.00

Membership is annual (calendar year) with renewal payment due 1st January each year.

*Note: Junior membership is conditional upon a parent or guardian becoming a member who must accompany the junior member whilst at the club and whilst at club functions.
