About This Course

Explore the art of rock carving and learn how to shape and sculpt stone into stunning works of art. This hands-on course covers everything from selecting the right tools to mastering carving techniques.

  • Hands-on practice with carving tools
  • Techniques for shaping and detailing stone
  • Finishing and polishing your carvings
