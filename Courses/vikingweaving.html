Viking Weaving

Learn the ancient art of Viking wire weaving to create stunning jewellery and decorative pieces.

About This Course

Discover the ancient technique of Viking wire weaving, a method used to create intricate chains and jewellery. This hands-on course will teach you how to weave wire into beautiful, durable designs.

  • Hands-on practice with wire weaving techniques
  • Creating chains, bracelets, and decorative pieces
  • Finishing and polishing your woven designs

Prerequisites

  • No prior experience required
  • Basic knowledge of tools is helpful but not necessary
  • All materials and tools will be provided
