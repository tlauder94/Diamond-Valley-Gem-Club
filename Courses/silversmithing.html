Silversmithing

Master the art of crafting beautiful silver jewellery and decorative pieces.

About This Course

Learn the fundamentals of silversmithing, from shaping and soldering silver to creating stunning jewellery and decorative items. This hands-on course is perfect for beginners and those looking to refine their skills.

  • Hands-on practice with silversmithing tools
  • Techniques for soldering, shaping, and polishing silver
  • Designing and creating custom silver pieces

Prerequisites

  • No prior experience required
  • Basic knowledge of tools is helpful but not necessary
  • All materials and tools will be provided
