About This Course

Learn the art of shaping and polishing rocks and gemstones into smooth, domed cabochons. This hands-on course covers everything from selecting your rough to using lapidary equipment safely and effectively. Perfect for beginners or anyone looking to level up their rock game.

  • Hands-on practice with lapidary tools
  • Techniques for selecting and preparing rough stones
  • Polishing and finishing techniques

Prerequisites

  • No prior experience required
  • Basic knowledge of tools is helpful but not necessary
  • All materials and tools will be provided
