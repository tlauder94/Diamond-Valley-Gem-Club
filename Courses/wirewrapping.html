About This Course

Master the art of wire wrapping, a versatile technique used to create intricate jewellery and decorative pieces. This hands-on course will teach you how to manipulate wire to create unique designs.

  • Hands-on practice with wire wrapping techniques
  • Creating pendants, rings, and decorative designs
  • Finishing and polishing your wire-wrapped creations
