About This Course

Learn the art of jewellery making, from designing your own pieces to mastering essential techniques like soldering, stone setting, and polishing. This course is perfect for beginners and hobbyists alike.

  • Hands-on practice with jewellery-making tools
  • Techniques for soldering and stone setting
  • Designing and creating custom jewellery

Prerequisites

  • No prior experience required
  • Basic knowledge of tools is helpful but not necessary
  • All materials and tools will be provided
