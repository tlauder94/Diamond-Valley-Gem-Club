Lost Wax Casting

Master the ancient art of lost wax casting to create intricate and unique metal designs.

About This Course

Learn the ancient technique of lost wax casting, a process used to create intricate metal designs. This course will guide you through every step, from carving your wax model to casting it in metal and finishing your final piece.

  • Hands-on practice with wax carving and casting
  • Techniques for creating intricate designs
  • Metal finishing and polishing

Prerequisites

  • No prior experience required
  • Basic knowledge of tools is helpful but not necessary
  • All materials and tools will be provided
